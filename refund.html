REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY – THE ROUND ROOM

Since all products are freshly prepared, we do not accept returns under any circumstances.

Cancellations

  • Orders may be cancelled only if the request is made at least 24 hours before dispatch.
  • Cancellation approval is at the sole discretion of The Round Room.
  • Approved cancellations may be refunded or converted to store credit.
  • Orders cannot be cancelled once preparation or dispatch has begun.

Refunds

Refunds are only applicable if:

  • The order is not delivered
  • The product is damaged or incorrect

Customers must contact us within 24 hours of delivery with:

  • Order number
  • Clear photos of product and packaging

Approved refunds will be credited to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

No refunds will be issued for:

  • Change of mind
  • Incorrect address provided by customer
  • Delays caused by customer unavailability
