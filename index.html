OUR STORY

We craft handmade New York-style bagels, slow-whipped schmears, and dessert pots that feel familiar yet considered. Every element is made to be shared, from weekend pop-ups to curated home deliveries designed for moments that feel personal, not rushed. The Round Room isn't just about what's on the table. It's about creating small pauses in the day, where food becomes a reason to gather, talk, and stay a little longer.

With love,
Jia Kini
Founder, The Round Room
